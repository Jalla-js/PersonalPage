About Us

Welcome to Matt's Guitars, where passion for music and craftsmanship come together to create an exceptional experience for guitar enthusiasts. Our journey began over a decade ago with a simple love for guitars and a desire to provide high-quality instruments and services to musicians of all levels.

At Matt's Guitars, our mission is to inspire and support the musical journeys of our customers. We believe that every guitarist deserves an instrument that not only sounds great but feels right in their hands. Whether you're a seasoned professional or just starting out, our goal is to ensure you find the perfect guitar that resonates with your style and aspirations.

FAQ's

How long will it take to receive my order? Delivery times vary depending on your location and the shipping method selected. Typically, orders are delivered within 3-7 business days.
What is your return policy? We offer a 30-day return policy on most items. Products must be returned in their original condition. Certain items may be non-returnable.
How can I change or cancel my order? If you need to change or cancel your order, please contact us as soon as possible. If the order has not been processed, we will do our best to accommodate your request.
What do I do if I receive a damaged or incorrect item? If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, please contact us immediately.
Do you offer customization options for your products? Yes, some of our products can be customized. Please check the product details or contact us for more information.
How do I sign up for your newsletter? You can sign up for our newsletter by entering your email address at the bottom of our homepage
What should I do if I have a question that is not listed here? If your question is not listed here, please reach out to us directly via our contact page, and we'll be happy to assist you.
